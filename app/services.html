Umbrella Company

An ideal step into contracting is working through an umbrella company; a separate company that acts as an employer for contractors working on fixed-term contracts.
Team up with us and we’ll be with you each step of the way, with employment support and benefits that are second to none.

We’ll pay you swiftly and accurately while handling your administration, calculating your tax and NI contributions and making payments to HMRC..

Limited Company Accountancy

Going Limited is one of the most tax-efficient and flexible ways to work for yourself with benefits.

Be the Boss of you with your own Limited Liability Company and pay yourself a combination of salary and Dividends of up to 18% more by choosing limited in place of umbrella.

We offer expert accounting support to contractors working through limited companies.
